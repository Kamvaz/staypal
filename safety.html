Community Safety & Legal Housing Standards

At StayPal, your safety is not just a feature — it's a foundation. Founded by a former police officer, our platform is built to reflect accountability, lawful housing, and protection for both tenants and landlords.

✔️ Staypal Verified Listings

All verified listings carry a registered landlord number or location watermark on images to prevent scams and duplication.

📄 Legal Compliance

We ensure that all property uploads comply with local bylaws, zoning, and rental conditions as defined by South African law.

📸 Screenshot Protection

StayPal blocks screenshots and watermark images to protect our landlords' property visuals from being reused without consent.

🛡️ Community Reporting

Tenants can report suspicious listings or landlord misconduct directly via our in-app report system linked to local authorities.

👮 Liaison with Law Enforcement

We make it easy to coordinate with recognized and authorized law enforcement services to resolve safety disputes and verify legitimacy.

🔐 Secure Receipts & Records

All payments generate official receipts with tenant and landlord info, including registration IDs and date-stamped proofs.
